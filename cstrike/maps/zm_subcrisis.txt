Suburban Crisis - Zombie Mod

by Emil Georgiev (aka ZPhugeFan)

Story: The plague has quickly spread through the
entire city. Few are the survivors. In a wrecked house in the
city suburbs a few people are trying to withstand the zombie hordes
that are closing in...

Humans: Fight and survive the horror.

Zombies: Take over the world.

Other notes: There are explosive barrels with a huge magnitude.
Shoot them from afar.

(Press Fire to Continue)
